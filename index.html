EnergyWallet

App.

To enable transition to net zero society, we in the UK must fully refurbish our housing stock, which in its majority is poorly constructed and hardly insulated. To help push this agenda forward, the UK government has launched several schemes (e.g., Green Homes Grants, etc.). However, many householders have neither the skills, time, nor the knowledge to access the available funding and to plan and undertake the necessary retrofitting activates for their homes. This is particularly true for those in energy poverty. This project will explore a software-supported systems integration solution to address this challenge. Click here to visit the University's project page.

Features.

See how other users are using EnergyWallet. Before you sign up to EnergyWallet you can explore the app by using one of the mock user accounts.

See which grants you're eligible for. Complete the turn2us benefits calculator and link your results to Energy Wallet to keep track of what you're entitled to.

View your EPC certificate. Just tell us your postcode and we'll find your EPC certificate. Now you can see which retrofit activities need to be done.

Fence off some of your weekly income. Fence off some of your income for home improvement use only.

Locate a local merchant to complete your retrofit activity. Find the highest rated merchants in the area to carry out your retrofit activity.

Keep a history of your retrofit and loan activity. Use EnergyWallet to keep history of any retrofit activity or loans that you've taken.
